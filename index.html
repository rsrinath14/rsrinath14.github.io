About Me

I am a first-year MS student in Computer Vision at Carnegie Mellon University, where I also serve as a Research Assistant in the Human Sensing Lab under Prof. Fernando De la Torre. Previously, I worked as an AI Research Scientist at UnscriptAI, focusing on realistic talking head synthesis with advanced 3D vision models. My research journey has also led me to the Vision and AI Lab at IISc, where I was advised by Prof. Venkatesh Babu, and I’ve had the privilege of collaborating with Prof. Srinath Sridhar at Brown University and the Data Labs team at Capital One. My work primarily delves into advanced 3D representations, including Gaussian Splatting and Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF).
